Manage Product Feeds for Worldwide Shopping Market Places Effectively

Looking for an Odoo Product Feed App that allows you to upload your product feeds to numerous shopping search engines? AppJetty has come up with an exceptional app for Odoo store owners that lets them submit their product feeds to various global search engines like Google Shopping, Amazon, Shopping.com, Yahoo Shopping and more.

The Odoo Advanced Data Feed Manager App enables you to produce an XML file, CSV file, and TXT file with Product, Header and Footer patterns that you can directly upload on Google shopping data feed, Amazon shopping feed, Bing shopping feed, Yahoo shopping feed, etc.

Features

Multiple File Extensions

Now you will be able to select the file extension out of XML, CSV, and TXT to generate your feed in.

Google RSS Feed Support

Odoo Advanced Data Feed Manager App also has Google RSS feed support. Using this feature, you can choose to display an RSS feed on your website.

Multi-Language Support

Get to choose the language you want to display your product feed in on a search engine.

Multi-Website Support

Get to select any of Now select any of your multiple sites you want to generate product feeds for.

Feed Selection

Get to choose any of the three - Products, Internal Category and E-Commerce platform for the product feed generation.

Automated Scheduling of Data Feeds

Get to choose between auto-scheduling of the generation of XML, CSV and TXT files or manual scheduling of data feeds from system settings.
