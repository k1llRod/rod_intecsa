In default, odoo only allows you to select a warehouse on sale order, when the sale order is confirmed, all sale order lines will be picked up from your selected warehouse.
With this app, you are able to choose one or multi warehouses on the sale order line, and sale order line will be picked up from separately warehouse.

List features:

1. Choose one warehouse on sale order line.

2. Choose multi warehouse on sale order line.

3. Automatically suggest warehouses which are sufficient stock.

4. Stock Picking is created separately base on warehouse on sale order line.

1. Example 1: Choose one warehouse on sale order line.

Example 1: Choose one warehouse on sale order line

In sale order line, you are able to choose only one warehouse, that meaning is the product will be picked from your chose warehouse
In sale order, select option "Choose one warehouse on sale order line.", this is also default option, when you create order from sale order.


For example, You have 2 warehouses
And your available quantity of product "Desk Organizer" and "Large Cabinet" are same as below image


Available quantity of product "Desk Organizer" in WH_1 is 3 and in WH_2 is 4
Available quantity of product "Large Cabinet" in WH_1 is 2 and in WH_2 is 3


Now come back to sale order, if you select "Desk Organizer", it will suggest you should pick up stock from Warehouse 2. Because its available stock is greater than Warehouse 1.

You are able to select another warehouse by click on warehouse and select another one.

Select warehouse 1, if you would like to pick up stock from warehouse 1. Add "Large Cabinet" product and would like to pick up stock from warehouse 2
Your completed sale order as below

After you confirm sale order. 2 pickings will be created.

Example 3: Modify quantity on "Choose multi warehouse on sale order line" option

When you create a new sale order, select product and input quantity, this app will suggest the warehouse which you will pick up stock

After save sale order. it will show stock number you will pick up at the warehouse

Click on "Warehouses" button to modify stock quantity
For example, you want to pick up 1 stocks at the warehouse 1 and 5 stocks at the warehouse 2
The meaning of the red line is the warehouse 2 is not sufficient stock. Click on "Save" button and sale order will be updated.

Or you can add more warehouse.

Click on "Save" button and sale order will be updated.

After Confirm sale order, 3 delivery stock pickings will be created.
